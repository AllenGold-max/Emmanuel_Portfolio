Data Science Job Market EDA Using Python

This project provided valuable insights into what companies seek in a data science candidate. As an individual aspiring to break into data science and pursue a career as a Data Analyst, these are the key takeaways:

1. Knowledge of Python, R, and SQL: Proficiency in these programming languages and tools is crucial.
2. Educational Qualifications: Most job postings prefer candidates with a master's degree, making it advisable to pursue higher education for better job security.
3. Work Experience: The average required work experience is 4-5 years. Thus, finding an entry-level analyst role or internship and working on more projects will help meet this requirement.
