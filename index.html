📊 Data Science Job Market EDA | Python

Project Overview

This project provides valuable insights into the skills, qualifications, and experience that companies seek in a Data Science candidate. By analyzing job postings, it helps aspiring Data Analysts and Data Scientists understand industry expectations and career pathways.

Key Findings & Insights

✔ Programming Skills – Proficiency in Python, R, and SQL is essential for data science roles.
✔ Educational Qualifications – A Master’s degree is preferred in most job postings, making higher education a strategic advantage.
✔ Work Experience – The average required experience is 4-5 years; gaining internships, entry-level analyst roles, or personal projects can help bridge this gap.

Tools & Technologies Used

🔹 Python – Data extraction and analysis
🔹 Pandas & NumPy – Data manipulation and processing
🔹 Matplotlib & Seaborn – Data visualization for key trends

Project Impact

This project helps job seekers strategically position themselves by identifying industry demands and tailoring their skills, education, and experience accordingly. It serves as a data-driven guide to navigating the competitive job market in Data Science.
